About us

We are Federally Reef and Pelagic 6 pack (persons) Gulf of Mexico charter and we carry full insurance and liability on the boat. We have a Liferaft aboard and take safety very seriously. I am CPR and First aid trained for both adults and infants and I have experience rescuing a firefighter who Drowned while diving on a friends commercial boat I was diving on and I was able to get him breathing and revive him after 20 minutes or so and he fully recovered. October 13th 2018. I got a front page article for us and our efforts as he was blue and not breathing.
